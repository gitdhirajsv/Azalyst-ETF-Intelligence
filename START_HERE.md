## 🛑 To Disable Auto-Start

### Method 1: Delete Shortcut
1. Press `Win+R`
2. Type: `shell:startup`
3. Delete `Azalyst ETF Intelligence.lnk`

### Method 2: Use Task Scheduler
1. Press `Win+R`
2. Type: `taskschd.msc`
3. Find "Azalyst ETF Intelligence"
4. Right-click → Disable
